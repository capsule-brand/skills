Internal Claude skills for the Capsule team. Download one, add it to the Claude app, then just talk to it.

Official Capsule tools, maintained internally
 

How to install any skill

  1. Download the skill below (a .skill file).
  2. Open the Claude app and go to Settings → Claude Cowork → Upload skill.
  3. Pick the file you just downloaded. The skill shows up in your list, and you use it by saying what you want or typing its slash command.
Something not working, or want a new skill? Ask Brian or post in #design on Slack. capsule-brand/skills
